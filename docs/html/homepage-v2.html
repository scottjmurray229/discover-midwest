Festivals

Plan Around the Party

The Philippines throws the best festivals in Southeast Asia. Our AI planner detects events during your travel dates automatically.

Jan 19, 2027

Sinulog Festival

Cebu City · Visayas

Nine days of drumbeats, dancing, and devotion honoring the Santo Niño. The biggest festival in the Visayas.
Jan 2027

Ati-Atihan

Kalibo, Aklan · Visayas

The mother of all Filipino festivals. Face paint, tribal costumes, and a three-day street party.
Feb 2027

Panagbenga

Baguio City · Luzon

Massive floral floats wind through the cool mountain city, celebrating the blooming season.
May 15, 2027

Pahiyas Festival

Lucban, Quezon · Luzon

Houses decorated with colorful kiping (rice wafers) and vegetables in gratitude for the harvest.
Oct 2027

MassKara Festival

Bacolod City · Visayas

The City of Smiles lives up to its name with elaborate masks, street dances, and electric energy.
